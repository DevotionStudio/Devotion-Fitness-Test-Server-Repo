How it works

Five things, one app.

Plan, train, eat, recover, repeat. Devotion handles the loop so you can focus on showing up.

  • 01 · Plan

    A plan that fits you, today.

    Tell us your goal — cutting, bulking, just stronger — and the app builds your week. Adjusts as life happens.

  • 02 · Train

    Track every set, fast.

    Big buttons, big numbers. Logs sets in seconds. Knows your last lift, your PR, your bar speed.

  • 03 · Coach

    An AI in your corner.

    Asks if a set felt easy. Suggests alternates when the rack is busy. Pushes you when you're flat.

  • 04 · Eat

    Calories that just work.

    Search any food in two taps. Macros land in your daily ring. Cutting or bulking — targets adapt.

  • 05 · Recover

    Earn your rest days.

    Body readiness scoring per muscle. Banked rest days for streaks. No more guilt-skipping leg day.
